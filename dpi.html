DPI Tester

Instructions

1. Enter the distance you plan to travel into the "Distance" text input below.
2. Lay a ruler down on a flat surface.
3. Align the center of your mouse with the start of an inch value on the ruler.
4. Click the black box below to lock your cursor into the tracker.
5. Click again to begin testing.
6. Using the ruler as a guide, move your mouse that exact distance you entered to the best of your ability.

NOTE: To redo the test, YOU MUST REFRESH THE PAGE. This is to ensure the browser's mouse state is reset.

For most accurate results make sure...
1. Mouse acceleration is turned OFF (Instructions Here).
2. Do your best to move the mouse in whole inches (aligned with center of mouse).
3. Ensure Hardware acceleration is turned ON in your Browser Settings.
